The Art of Perfect Coffee Brewing

Making the perfect cup of MayMyo Fresh coffee is both an art and a science. Follow these steps to achieve the best results:

1. Water Temperature

The ideal water temperature for brewing coffee is between 195°F and 205°F (90-96°C). Boil your water and let it rest for about 30 seconds before brewing.

2. Coffee-to-Water Ratio

For our premium Arabica blend, we recommend:

  • Strong brew: 2 tablespoons per 6 oz of water
  • Medium brew: 1.5 tablespoons per 6 oz of water
  • Light brew: 1 tablespoon per 6 oz of water

3. Brewing Time

For the best results with our 3-in-1 coffee:

  1. Add hot water to your cup
  2. Pour one sachet of MayMyo Fresh coffee
  3. Stir gently for 15-20 seconds
  4. Let it rest for 30 seconds before enjoying

Pro Tip

For the best experience, use filtered water and always store your coffee in an airtight container away from direct sunlight.
