History of Pyin Oo Lwin Coffee

November 15, 2023 By Local Historian

The Rich Heritage of Coffee in Pyin Oo Lwin

Pyin Oo Lwin, formerly known as Maymyo, has a rich history of coffee cultivation dating back to the British colonial era.

Colonial Beginnings

Coffee was first introduced to Pyin Oo Lwin in the late 19th century by British colonists who recognized the region's perfect climate and soil conditions for coffee cultivation.

Modern Development

Today, Pyin Oo Lwin is Myanmar's coffee capital, producing some of the finest Arabica beans in Southeast Asia. The unique combination of altitude, climate, and soil creates coffee with distinct characteristics:

  • Rich, full-bodied flavor
  • Subtle chocolate notes
  • Balanced acidity
  • Smooth finish

Cultural Significance

Coffee farming has become an integral part of Pyin Oo Lwin's cultural identity, supporting local communities and preserving traditional farming methods.
